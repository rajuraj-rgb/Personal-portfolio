Hello, I'm Raju Rajbongshi, and I'm an Analyst by profession. I've always been passionate about troubleshooting and problem-solving, and I've spent the last few years honing my skills and gaining experience in this field.
I completed my schooling from Aaryan International School with a strong foundation in science and maths. Currently, I'm pursuing my B.Sc. in Design and Computing to enhance my knowledge of the latest technologies and design principles.
I'm always eager to learn and explore new technologies, which is why I spend my free time developing my skills in various areas like web development, app development, and video editing. I also run a YouTube channel where I share my experiences and knowledge on various technical topics.
At the core of my work is a commitment to excellence and a willingness to go the extra mile to ensure success. I believe in collaborating effectively with my team members and continuously improving my skills to deliver high-quality work.
Thank you for taking the time to learn more about me. If you have any questions or would like to learn more about my work, please don't hesitate to get in touch.
